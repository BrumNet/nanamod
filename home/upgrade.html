Trade-In Policy for Tractors

At OX ENGINEERING LIMITED, we value our customers and their evolving needs. That’s why we offer a flexible Trade-In Program for your old tractors, making it easier and more affordable to upgrade to a new, more efficient model. Our trade-in process is simple, transparent, and designed to offer you the best value.



How Our Trade-In Program Works

  1. Submit Your Trade-In Request: Start by filling out our online form with details about your current tractor, including the make, model, year, condition, and any attachments or upgrades. You can also bring your tractor into any of our locations for an on-site evaluation.

  2. Receive a Fair Offer: Based on the information provided, we’ll assess the value of your tractor and present you with a competitive trade-in offer. Our experts consider factors like age, condition, hours of use, and market demand to ensure you get a fair deal.

  3. Apply Your Trade-In Credit: Once you accept the offer, you can apply the trade-in credit toward the purchase of a new tractor or any other product from our range. This credit helps reduce the upfront cost of upgrading to a newer, more powerful, and fuel-efficient model.

  4. Upgrade to a New Tractor: After applying your trade-in value, you can browse our selection of tractors and choose the model that best fits your current needs. We offer financing options to help make your upgrade even more accessible.



Why Trade In with [OX ENGINEERING LIMITED]?

  • Fair Market Value: We offer competitive trade-in values based on market conditions, ensuring you get the best possible deal for your old tractor.

  • Hassle-Free Process: Our trade-in process is fast, straightforward, and handled by our team of professionals, saving you time and effort.

  • Upgrade to the Latest Technology: Get the most out of your farm operations by upgrading to a newer tractor with enhanced features, better fuel efficiency, and more power.

  • Environmentally Friendly: Trading in your old tractor allows us to refurbish and recycle parts, contributing to a more sustainable farming ecosystem.



What Tractors Are Eligible for Trade-In?

We accept a wide variety of tractors in our trade-in program, regardless of make or model. Whether your current tractor is still in excellent condition or has seen better days, we’ll evaluate its value and offer a fair trade-in credit.
